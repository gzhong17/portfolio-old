ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Gloria. Based in Vancouver, BC, I specialize in UX & UI design as well as front-end development. I firmly believe in the importance of "considerate design".

Consideration for others often goes unnoticed but in its absence, people become confused and distraught.

As a designer and developer, it is my responsibility to put my users' best interests at heart, being attentive to details, and simplifying unnecessary components.

Through studying in Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, I have grown to enjoy designing and building interfaces and websites.
